Rank Title: Degree: ?
You can use the degree of a rank to specify which ranks are above and below each other. A rank with a higher degree is considered above a rank with a lower degree. For example, if you are a degree 10 rank, and have the permission to boot someone from your clan, you can boot anyone with a degree of 9 or lower, but not anyone with a rank of 11 or more. (It's ridiculous.) Degrees only apply to booting members, changing members' ranks, or changing members' titles.
