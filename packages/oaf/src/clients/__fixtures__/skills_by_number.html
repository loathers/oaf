Notes[edit]

  • Jick usually avoids the number 13, but strangely there is a skill 13: Gnomish Hardigness. Several others end in 13, such as 6013: Stevedave's Shanty of Superiority and 7013: Fire Purple Bottle-Rocket.
  • Although, there do appear to be no skills in the 13000-13999 range. Avatar of Boris, Zombie Slayer and Avatar of Jarlsberg were the first 3 new classes as part of the special challenge paths.
  • From the account menu auto-attack settings, skill 0 is auto-attack disabled, 1 is attack with your weapon, and 3 is pickpocket.
  • Although skill number 6019 is in the Accordion Thief listing, it is not an Accordion Thief skill, although the resultant buff does count towards your maximum song count.
  • Skill numbers 990000 - 999999 appear to be reserved for Combat Macros.
